About the League

The League of Gentlemen provides a network of individuals from high school and onward who are all committed to developing a positive impact on the world, a relationship and access to professionals of all pedigrees, as well as personal, professional, and academic development in an organization that holds prestige.

Every member of the League of Gentlemen has taken a pledge to the advance the world. The pledge is open to all people. If you are willing to take on a responsibility in changing the world you can repeat this pledge as well:

I pledge allegiance to the world, filled with many diverse peoples and cultures; and to its development, as long as it stands, all nations, unified equitably, with love and equality for all.
